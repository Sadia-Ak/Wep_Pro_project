HTML Tags and Classes for the School Website

  • <header>: To include the school's logo and navigation bar.
  • <p>: For displaying text content and paragraphs.
  • Strong can be used in between the paragraph tag, to showcase importance of Professor, Syallbus, etc.
  • Table (table, tr, td) for timetables for the course schedule
  • <footer>: Footer for contact details and other important links.
  • Div and Span for grouping and inline elements, and the courses
  • <div>: Container to structure the webpage.
  • Image (img) for graphical content
  • List (ul, ol, li) for listing subjects, syllabuses, dates, etc.
  • Style can be used to give it a unique eye catching look
  • <section>: Divides the page into sections like assignments, teachers, etc.
  • Class .timetable for styling timetable content
